# Privacy Policy

**No BS TV browser**  
Last updated: June 9, 2026

## Summary

**We do not collect any data.**

No BS TV browser is built to stay out of your way. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share your personal information.

## What we do not collect

The app does not gather:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports sent to us
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking or advertising
- Browsing history sent to our servers

There are no accounts, sign-ups, or third-party analytics SDKs in the app.

## Data stored on your device

Some information stays **only on your device** so the browser can work:

- **Bookmarks** — titles and URLs you save, stored in local app storage
- **Browsing data** — cache, cookies, and site data managed by the WebView, same as any browser

This data never leaves your device unless you back up the app through Android’s backup system or you choose to share it yourself.

You can clear cache and site data at any time from Settings inside the app. Bookmarks are kept unless you delete them.

## Network access

The app needs internet access to load web pages you request. Those requests go directly to the websites you visit. We do not operate a proxy and do not receive a copy of your browsing activity.

Websites you visit may collect their own data according to their privacy policies. That is outside our control.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this file and the “Last updated” date above.
